About Synq'd:

Host or Join Session:

Whether you're the music maestro or just eager to vibe, Synq'd has you covered. Host a session with your Spotify Premium account or join one using your free or premium Spotify account.

Request and Vote:

Everyone's a DJ with Synq'd! Members of a session can request their favorite tunes and cast their votes. The power is in your hands – the more votes, the higher the chance of hearing that epic track.

Intuitive Interface:

Navigate through the app with ease. View the current song playing, take control with playback options (exclusive to hosts), and explore the host's queue. The list of requested songs displays the vote count, keeping the excitement alive.
